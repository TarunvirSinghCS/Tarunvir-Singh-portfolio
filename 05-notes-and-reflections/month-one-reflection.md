# Month One Reflection

Over the first five weeks of building this portfolio, I improved my ability to explain technical systems, troubleshoot problems, and visualize network communication.

The most valuable skill I developed was structured problem solving.
Documenting troubleshooting steps helped me think more logically about identifying root causes before jumping to solutions.

Creating the network diagram improved my understanding of how user devices communicate with internet services through routers, ISPs, and servers.

The security lab helped me understand the importance of firewalls, endpoint protection, and system updates.

Going forward, I want to continue improving my cybersecurity knowledge through hands-on labs and real-world troubleshooting scenarios.
